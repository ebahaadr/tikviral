Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TikViral collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our app:

  • Account Information: When you create an account (e.g., via Apple or Email), we collect basic profile information strictly to manage your account.
  • Subscription & Payment Data: We track your subscription status to provide premium features. Payments are securely processed by Apple; we do not access or store your raw payment details.
  • Usage and Device Data: We collect anonymous crash reports, telemetry, and performance data to improve app stability and user experience.
  • Media & Content: If you upload or interact with videos or images as part of the app's functionality, this data is processed to provide our services.
  • Push Notifications: If you opt-in, we securely store device tokens to send you relevant alerts and updates.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To authenticate users and provide cross-device access to accounts.
  • To process and manage your subscriptions and purchases.
  • To send administrative information and optional push notifications.
  • To identify, debug, and fix crashes or performance issues.

3. Third-Party Services

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers that assist us in operating our application:

  • Apple (for Authentication and In-App Purchases)
  • Firebase (for secure Authentication, Database, and Cloud Storage)
  • RevenueCat (for managing subscriptions and entitlements)
  • Sentry (for crash reporting, telemetry, and error tracking)

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data. You can delete your account directly within the app settings. Once an account is deleted, your personal data is permanently removed from our active databases.

5. Security of Your Information

We implement strict, industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our application and services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or Apple's guidelines. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
